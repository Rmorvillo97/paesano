Purpose

My purpose in this world is to do all that I can, and do it to the best of my ability. Spending countless hours volunteeing especially in places far from home will help give yourself an understanding of what it's like to live in other people's shoes. With Habitat for Humanity, here in Albany and North Carolina I have learned of struggles lower class families have suffered from, endured, and risen from. Abroad in Italy I have learned how hard it is for the immigrant populations and refugees settle in a land so foreign not understanding the language as well. I have myself in a way experienced the same thing because of the lack of speaking Italian. After time it got better but I always stuck out when trying to converse with local Italians. It is the responsibility of each and ever one of us to help one another as much as we can.
